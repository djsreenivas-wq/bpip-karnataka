# BPIP Karnataka
## Bharat Public Intelligence Platform

Version: 1.0

---

# Vision

To ensure every citizen of Karnataka receives verified, easy-to-understand government information at the right time.

---

# Mission

Bridge the gap between Government and Citizens using Artificial Intelligence, Knowledge Management and Digital Awareness.

# Platform Architecture

BPIP consists of three major platforms.

• Government Orders

• Vacancies

• Scholarships

• Subsidies

• Agriculture Advisories

• Weather Alerts

• Reservoir Updates

• Panchayat Announcements
